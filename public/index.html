Services 

Once you have agreed to participate in the Panel and have downloaded the facebook messenger application to your mobile device, we request you to search for and add the ‘Self Sourcing BOT” and type “Hello”, accordingly there will be a set of questions which will be shared with you and your responses shared on the interface will then be recorded. By sharing your responses you explicitly consent to Nielsen using the responses in the manner envisaged and required herein for the furtherance of the services performed by Nielsen.

 

Nielsen Survey’s Do Not Contain Any Spyware or Adware 

We do not serve pop-up or pop-under advertising through the facebook messenger application and we do not install hidden, ad-supported programs onto users' mobile phones. 

 

Collection and Use 

"Personally identifying information" means information that by itself – like your first and last name or personal email address – or in combination with other information could identify a specific individual. 

 

We receive information such as: 

* Name 

* Home Address 

* Work Address 

* Email Address 

* Phone Number 

* IMEI/IMSI/ESN 

* Date of Birth 

* Product details

 

An example of the information collected is as follows:

 

  • Pictures of Visicoolers which are the assets of the Soft drink and may be provided specifically by a particular company.
  • Pictures from the shelf display or window display.
  • Pictures of the company specific invoices and bills generated at the time of sales.

If you respond to survey questions that are presented to you as a result of your participation as a panelist, the information you provide will be collected and used. 

 

Nielsen does not listen to or record any of your phone calls. We also do not examine, store or record any of the contents of text or picture messages you send or receive, except as specifically shared with us. In addition, we do not read any of the contents of your other files or email messages stored on your mobile device. 

 

We use the information collected to perform research and to prepare aggregated reports and analyses about the concept of self-sourcing using a facebook chat. These include, for example, reports and analyses based on combined information that does not identify individual people. We may also use your behavior data to create statistical models of people that look like you and that we believe are likely to have the same interests and behaviors as you.

 

Sharing Your Information 

As noted above, we do not provide our clients with any of the personally identifying information or collect from metering your device, nor do we ever use this information for direct marketing purposes. We use tools and methods to make sure that there is no reasonable possibility of identifying an individual in the reports that we create for our clients. For example, we combine the data we collect from your mobile phone with the data of others to produce "aggregated" reports. 

 

Occasionally we work with other companies for the purposes of data processing or market research product development purposes. Companies that are given access to your information in connection with providing services for us are contractually required to keep that information confidential and are not permitted to use this information for any other purpose except to carry out the services that they are performing for us. 

 

We may release personally identifying information to third parties when disclosure is required by law or when necessary or desirable to exercise or preserve our legal rights, or to take action regarding potentially illegal activities, or to protect the safety or rights of any person or legal entity. 

 

If all or part of Nielsen is sold, merged or otherwise transferred to an unrelated entity, your information, whether personally identifying or otherwise, may be transferred as part of that transaction. 

 

Security 

The security of data and the protection of personal information are essential to our success and credibility. We have in place certain security measures, including encryption methods and access protocols, to help protect the security and confidentiality of personally identifying information while controlled by us. We store your information for as long as needed for our research and analyses purposes and in accordance with law. Your information may be stored in the United States or elsewhere but in any event will be stored using administrative, managerial and technical measures to protect its confidentiality. Please remember that storage and communication of personal data cannot always be one-hundred percent secure. 

 

Updates to This Privacy Notice 

We reserve the right to update and make changes to this Privacy Notice and to our practices in collecting and handling personally identifying information. If we make a material change (the kind of change to the way we use your information that might make you reconsider participating in this panel), we will notify you by either sending a message to the most recent email address you gave us or sending a message to your mobile phone via the facebook messenger. We will notify you at least 30 days before we plan to make a material change to give you time to resign from the panel if you want. 

Your continued membership in this panel after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of all of the changes.

About Nielsen 

Nielsen Holdings plc (NYSE: NLSN) is a global performance management company that provides a comprehensive understanding of what consumers Watch and Buy. Nielsen's Watch segment provides media and advertising clients with Total Audience measurement services across all devices where content - video, audio and text - is consumed. The Buy segment offers consumer packaged goods manufacturers and retailers the industry's only global view of retail performance measurement. By integrating information from its Watch and Buy segments and other data sources, Nielsen provides its clients with both world-class measurement as well as analytics that help improve performance. Nielsen, an S&P 500 company, has operations in over 100 countries that cover more than 90 percent of the world's population. For more information, please visit www.nielsen.com.
